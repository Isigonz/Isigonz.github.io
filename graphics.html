Evaluation of Web Graphics

Site evaluated: Adidas

Purpose of the graphics on this site

The graphics on the Adidas home page are large and very noticible. They take up all of the space in the home page. There is a large slide show banner at the top that shows images of different clothing. Below the banner are two smaller images next to each other that show a differnet line of clothing that they offer. As you scroll down, you see a smaller graphics that show you whats trending. Of course at the very top of the page, there is a navigation menu. when the navigation button is hovered over, a larger menu drops down wth more options. There is also a search bar on the top of the page.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics

  1. Yes-Graphics fit with the style,purpose, and organization of the page.
  2. Yes-Graphics were not too big in file size.
  3. Yes-Graphics grab the attention of viewers and help them focus on certain things in the page.
  4. Yes-Graphics do not repeat too much and become obnoxious.
  5. Yes-This site uses proper text to convey their message.
  6. Yes-Graphics provide text alternatives.
  7. Yes-Graphics use sufficient contrast between text and image.
